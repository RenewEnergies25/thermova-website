Heat pumps and Fleetwood housing stock

Fleetwood has a mix of inter-war bay-window semis, ex-council post-war stock, and newer estates closer to the bypass. Coastal weather exposure is a real factor — wind, salt, and lower average winter temperatures all matter for outdoor-unit specification.

Postcodes covered

Fleetwood sits inside the Thermova service area — FY7. Our base is Harbury, 33 Derby Road, Poulton-le-Fylde (FY6), so we're routinely on the road across Blackpool, Lytham, Fleetwood, Preston and the wider Fylde Coast.

Local planning & site considerations

Fleetwood is largely outside formal Conservation Areas, so Permitted Development typically applies. Outdoor-unit siting needs to consider both wind exposure and the salt environment — we default to marine-grade specifications on all FY-postcode installs.

Frequently asked questions

Do you actually install in Fleetwood?
Yes. Thermova's base is Harbury, 33 Derby Road, Poulton-le-Fylde (FY6 7AF) — Fleetwood (FY7) is in our core service area. Same team surveys and installs.
How fast can you get out to survey?
Typical survey lead time is one to two weeks from first call. Emergency situations (boiler failed, no heat) we'll prioritise where possible — call +44 7976 015890.
Do you handle the £7,500 grant on local installs?
Yes. The Boiler Upgrade Scheme grant is managed end-to-end on every eligible install. £7,500 is deducted directly from your invoice (or £9,000 if you're replacing oil or LPG heating, under the April 2026 amendment) — you don't fill in any forms with Ofgem.
What if my property has planning restrictions?
Most heat pump installs fall under Permitted Development in England and Wales. Conservation Areas and listed buildings need consent — we check at survey and route the planning application ourselves where it's needed.
How does your install differ from a national installer?
We design and install one property at a time, personally. Graham signs off every design. Our published Lytham case study shows the year-one performance numbers — measured, not modelled. You meet the people responsible for your install, not a salesperson.
What if I've had a quote elsewhere already?
Bring it. We're happy to second-opinion an existing quote — usually free, no obligation. The most common issue we see is undersized radiator changes for the design flow temperature.
