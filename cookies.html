Cookie Policy

Last updated: 23 April 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, understand how you use the site, and improve your experience. Cookies cannot run programs or deliver viruses to your device.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential cookies — necessary for the website to function correctly. These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies — provided by Google Analytics, these help us understand how visitors interact with the Website (pages visited, time on site, traffic sources). This data is aggregated and anonymous.
  • Functional cookies — remember preferences you set during your visit (for example, form field values).

The “What our customers say” section on our homepage is powered by a third-party Google Reviews widget. When that section loads, the widget provider may set its own cookies in your browser to deliver the reviews and measure usage of the widget. These cookies are set by the widget provider, not by Thermova, and are governed by that provider’s cookie policy.

4. How to Manage Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings. You can delete all cookies already on your device and set most browsers to prevent new ones from being placed. Note that if you do this, some features of the Website may not work as intended.

Instructions for the major browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

You can also opt out of Google Analytics tracking specifically using Google's opt-out browser add-on.

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes are effective when posted to this page, with the "last updated" date revised accordingly.
